## ğŸ”’ Best Practices

1. **Never trust raw GPS** - Always snap to road
2. **Route-based movement** - Bike follows polyline, not GPS
3. **Smooth interpolation** - No sudden jumps
4. **Speed limits** - Clamp between 10-45 km/h
